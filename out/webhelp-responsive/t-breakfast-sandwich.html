How to make a Breakfast Sandwich

A wonderful and nutritious breakfst sandwich to start your day off right.

Ingredients
  • 1 English muffin
  • 2 tablespoon soy vegan sausage, pressed into a round patty
  • 3 thin slices of bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegan shredded cheese
  • Â½ teaspoon vegan butter
  • pinch of black pepper
  • extra virgin olive oil for coating patty
  1. Slice 2 tablespoons of vegan sausage and form it into a round patty.
  2. Rub the patty in a very light amount of oil and place on grill.
  3. Slice open the English Muffin and press the open faces on the grill
  4. Rub sliced peppers in a little oil and place on the grill.
  5. Allow the patty, peppers, and muffin grill for a minute.
  6. Turn off the grill and sprinkle cheese over the top of the patties.
  7. Place the green peppers on top of cheese and let the patty sit on the grill until the cheese melts.
  8. Take the English Muffin and spread 1 teaspoon of butter on one side, and maple syrup and/or jam on the other side.
  9. Then put the sandwich together. Place the patty on top of bottom piece of muffin and put the muffin top on patty.
